1. Introduction

The solar energy market is expanding rapidly, with solar PV expected to remain at the forefront of renewable energy solutions. However, many companies, including EC Newenergie, still rely on manual tracking methods such as spreadsheets and phone calls to monitor project progress. This approach becomes increasingly inefficient as the number of simultaneous projects grows.

2. Problem Statement

Based on a preliminary interview with stakeholder Miss Aziah Binti Abdul Rahim (Assistant Project Manager) on 5 April 2025, the following issues were identified:

  • Absence of Centralized Tracking: Project managers must rely on manual updates via calls or emails, which is time-consuming and prone to miscommunication.
  • Delayed Decision Making: Without real-time updates, emerging issues like installation delays, material shortages, or manpower lack are not addressed promptly (Jamil et al., 2021).
  • Customer Dissatisfaction: Customers are often left uninformed about their installation status, leading to frustration and loss of trust (Serrador & Pinto, 2015).
  • Risk of Penalties: Manual processes increase the likelihood of missing critical deadlines and overlooking essential tasks.

3. Objectives

  • To gather the system requirements for a project management tracking system with dashboard capabilities for EC Newenergie.
  • To design a project management tracking system that includes a dashboard for monitoring and project overview.
  • To develop said tracking system for EC Newenergie to streamline operations.

4. Research Method

The project follows the Agile Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to ensure flexibility and continuous improvement.

  • Requirement Gathering: Interviews with stakeholders to identify pain points.
  • System Design: UML modeling and UI/UX prototyping.
  • Development:
    Backend: Laravel (PHP)
    Database: MySQL
    Frontend: Blade Templates & Tailwind CSS

Advanced Computational Logic:

  • Multi-Factor Weighted Progress: Unlike simple checklist systems, our "Overall Progress" is a computed metric weighting Physical Workflow (40%) against Financial Milestones (60%), giving stakeholders a truer picture of project health.
  • Automated Critical Path Analysis: The system automatically calculates "Delayed" or "At Risk" statuses by comparing real-time progress against a standard 196-day installation baseline.
  • Dynamic Financial Integration: Real-time syncing ensures that displayed contract values and outstanding balances reflect the latest approved Variation Orders (VOs) and processed payments.

6. Conclusion

The Project Management Tracking System successfully addresses the critical issues faced by EC Newenergie.

  • It eliminates the reliance on manual spreadsheets and phone calls.
  • It provides a centralized platform for real-time monitoring, reducing the risk of delays.
  • It enhances professional image and customer trust by ensuring transparent and timely progress updates.

Future work will focus on integrating mobile push notifications for on-site install teams.
